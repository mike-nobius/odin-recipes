Tahina

Sesame paste based sauce with lemon, garlic, and cumin. Great on salads and sandwiches, or served with veggie sticks or falafel for dipping.

  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup tahini
  • 2/3 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp cumin or more to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Water to desired thinness
  1. Grate garlic or smash into a paste with a garlic press
  2. Mix together tahini, lemon juice and cumin. This will initially thicken the tahini and you'll think you're doing something wrong
  3. Continue adding water and mixing until homogenous until the desired consistency is achieved. The add salt to taste
